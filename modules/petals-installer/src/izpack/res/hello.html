Welcome in Petals ESB Installer

This will install and set-up OW2 Petals Entreprise Service Bus on your system.

Petals ESB is fully Open Source (LGPL) and is developed by the Petals team petals-dev@ow2.org. You can find out more about Petals ESB on the project website at http://petals.ow2.org/.
